## About Gigly

Gigly is a healthcare workforce marketplace and shift management platform. We help hospitals and health systems close short-term, extra, and overtime staffing gaps using their own eligible staff - rather than relying on costly agencies or last-minute scrambling.

Administrators and managers post open shifts, match them to qualified employees by department and specialty, configure incentives, and notify the right people. Staff discover and accept eligible extra shifts straight from the mobile app. Everyone gains visibility into fill rates, cancellations, incentives, and staffing performance.

## How It Works

- **Post** - Managers and administrators publish open short-term, extra, and overtime shifts.
- **Match** - Shifts are surfaced to eligible staff based on organization, department, specialization, clinical role, and overtime eligibility.
- **Notify** - Qualified employees receive in-app and push notifications when relevant shifts open up.
- **Accept** - Staff pick up full or partial shifts from the mobile app, with remaining time re-released as new eligible shifts.
- **Track** - Administrators monitor fill rates, cancellations, incentives, and staff participation through operational dashboards and reports.

## Built For Healthcare Environments

Gigly is designed for the realities of healthcare staffing, where needs vary by department, specialty, location, contract rules, and employee eligibility. It supports multi-organization accounts - parent health systems and their child organizations - with role-based workflows:

| Role                               | What they do                                                                              |
|------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Platform administrators**        | Manage organizations, users, wage benchmarks, configuration, and system-level reporting.  |
| **Parent / global administrators** | Oversee multiple child organizations under a parent account or health system.             |
| **Organization administrators**    | Manage departments, staff, specializations, contracts, and reporting for an organization. |
| **Managers & supervisors**         | Create, edit, cancel, and reopen shifts; monitor fill status and department performance.  |
| **Staff (mobile users)**           | View eligible shifts, accept full or partial work, and manage their participation.        |
